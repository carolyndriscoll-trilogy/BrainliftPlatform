    • Alpha High School Program Overview
      • Alpha’s Core Belief
        • Students learn best when they love school.
        • Mastery—not seat time or course count—is what creates confidence, motivation, and results.
        • Alpha students learn faster than students in standard school while still ranking in the top 1% nationwide- 2 times typical learning in only 3 hours.
      • The Uncomfortable Truth About High School
        • Traditional schools confuse instructional time with learning achieved.
        • Because of this factory model, most students don’t love school, they barely tolerate it, and are academically unprepared and disengaged.
        • Grade levels and overloaded transcripts are poor proxies for learning.
      • Alpha Flips the Typical Academic Model
        • Coursework is aligned to what top colleges actually care about: strong academic signals (SAT) and performance.
        • A precise knowledge map is created for each student based on what each student knows and needs, accelerating or filling holes when needed so gaps are fixed early, reducing frustration, and acceleration becomes possible.
        • In core subjects, Alpha students currently learn 3 grade levels a week, covering entire grade levels of coursework in a fraction of the time ( average of 6 weeks vs 18 weeks)
        • Instruction is targeted and time is flexible; as students learn faster, it gives them Time Back for life skills they care about.
      • Accredited, High-Efficiency Learning
        • Students are enrolled in Cognia accredited high school academic courses like Geometry, Biology, Algebra, and APs.
        • High school coursework is intentionally optimized for deep mastery of the skills that drive high SAT performance.
        • Concrete Examples: Traditional HS vs. Alpha HS
          • Math
            • Traditional High School: You take four years of math in a fixed order that everyone follows, covering many topics that don’t meaningfully improve SAT scores—despite SAT math being one of the most important signals for elite colleges.
            • Alpha High School: You take an SAT-crush math pathway that focuses on the exact skills tested on the SAT, while filling only the math foundations you personally need, leading to higher SAT scores.
          • Geometry
            • Traditional High School: Geometry is ~1 hour a day for ~180 school days, plus homework—no matter how much you already know.
            • Alpha High School: You take a Geometry diagnostic at the start, learn only what’s missing, and retest later. Students improve scores faster than in any traditional Geometry course.
          • Biology vs. AP Biology
            • Traditional High School: Regular Biology is riborous enough and if there is time (or even offered), take AP Biology.
            • Alpha High School: The difference between HS Biology and AP Biology is often ~40 hours. With strong foundations, those 40 hours unlock college credit and a potential AP score of 5.
      • The Proof
        • 90% of Alpha High School students love school.
        • Alpha students consistently learn 2× faster than peers based on nationally normed NWEA MAP assessment results.
        • Alpha High School SAT average is 1410 and climbing, compared to a national average near 1024.
        • The first wave of Alpha graduates have already been admitted to top universities like Stanford.
