3. Small Refinements That Matter
Use color to encode meaning, not decoration

Blue = navigation / structure

Green = verified

Amber = tension

Red = invalid

Never mix semantics.

Reduce saturation on analytics screens

Anything with data tables should stay mostly grayscale with small injections of color.

White space > color

Increase padding before adding color emphasis.
